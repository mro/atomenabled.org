What can AtomEnabled do for publishers?
AtomEnabling your website makes it easy for your readers to access your data through a syndication or aggregation program, giving them a consistent way to read your site. Readers are aware of updates the moment they’re available, and that immediacy strengthens the audience connection to your brand and content. Plus, you’re given more flexible control over the presentation and updating of your content publishing, allowing you to focus on your words and images instead of the details of technology used to distribute your publication.
